# Tone of Voice

pondex communicates like a knowledgeable friend who happens to understand finance — direct, honest, never condescending.

---

## Core Attributes

**Direct** — Say what you mean. No hedging, no corporate filler.  
**Honest** — If data is missing, say so. If confidence is low, say so. Don't dress up uncertainty as confidence.  
**Calm** — Not excited, not alarming. The user should feel clarity, not urgency.  
**Precise** — Specific language over vague language. "27 weighted factors" not "many parameters."  
**Unpretentious** — No jargon unless it's explained. "Free cash flow (FCF)" not just "FCF."

---

## Writing Rules

**Do:**
- Use active voice: "pondex calculates" not "the score is calculated"
- State uncertainty directly: "This score is based on limited data (Confidence: 52%)"
- Explain why, not just what: "Gross margin is 61% — above the 50% threshold that typically indicates pricing power"
- Use numbers: "3 conditions" not "several conditions"

**Don't:**
- Use marketing superlatives: "incredible," "revolutionary," "game-changing"
- Write "smart" or "intelligent" to describe the product — show it, don't say it
- Use "we" in product copy — pondex speaks as a tool, not a company
- Claim certainty that doesn't exist: "This stock will perform well" → never

## UI Copy Examples

| ❌ Avoid | ✓ Use |
|---|---|
| "Strong Buy" | "Excellent Fit for your Growth profile" |
| "AI-powered insights" | "Explanation based on live financial data" |
| "Comprehensive analysis" | "Score across 5 categories, 27 data points" |
| "We think this stock looks promising" | "Score: 4.1/5 — 18% below estimated fair value" |
| "Smart recommendations" | "Suggestions matched to your strategy profile" |
| "Data not available" | "Insider data: not available for non-US stocks" |

---

## Disclaimer Language

Every AI output, score, and suggestion must include or be accompanied by:

> "Algorithmic signal based on financial data. Not financial advice. Always verify with your own research."

Keep it short. Don't hide it. Don't over-legalize it.
