## Key Observations

1. **No free tool offers strategy-relative scoring.** This is pondex's clearest short-term differentiator.

2. **No tool closes the decision cycle.** Every competitor ends at "here's the data" or "here's an opinion." None track whether the user's thesis held up.

3. **Privacy gap.** Every competitor requires an account. pondex requires nothing — that's a genuine differentiator for a significant subset of users.

4. **AI chat exists (Finchat) but at $468/year** and without portfolio/thesis context. pondex's AI Chat with live data + profile context is meaningfully different.

---

## Competitive Risks

- **Seeking Alpha** could add a free strategy-profile filter — they have the data and distribution
- **Yahoo Finance** is owned by Apollo and could invest in AI features with their scale
- **Finchat** is growing and reducing price — could become a free-tier threat
- **OpenAI** / **Perplexity** could build financial data integrations that undercut the AI Chat value prop

**pondex's defense:** Thesis tracking + decision journal create switching cost that data-only or chat-only tools cannot replicate.
