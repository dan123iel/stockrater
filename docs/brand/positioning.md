# Positioning

## The Positioning Statement

For self-directed retail investors who analyze individual stocks, pondex is a decision intelligence platform that scores every stock relative to their personal strategy and tracks their investment thesis over time. Unlike Yahoo Finance or Seeking Alpha, pondex interprets data in the context of who you are as an investor — and doesn't stop at the buy button.

---

## What pondex Is

A system that makes investment decisions better — before the purchase, during holding, and at the sell.

---

## What pondex Is Not

Not a data provider. Not a trading platform. Not financial advice. Not a social network. Not a tool designed to increase screen time.

---

## The One-Line Pitch

> "pondex tells you if a stock fits your strategy — and reminds you why you bought it."

---

## Key Messages

**For the analysis moment:**
> "Not just what the numbers are — what they mean for you."

**For the thesis tracking moment:**
> "You documented your thesis six months ago. Here's whether it still holds."

**For the privacy angle:**
> "No account. No server. Your portfolio never leaves your browser."

**For the positioning vs. competitors:**
> "Yahoo Finance shows you data. pondex tells you what it means for your strategy."
